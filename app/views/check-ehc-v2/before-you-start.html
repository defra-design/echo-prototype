Before you start

If your certificate has a 12 digit number starting with a number 3, use the service from the Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs (DAERA) instead.

If your certificate does not have a 12 digit number, you cannot use this service. This includes certificates printed on crown gold paper.
