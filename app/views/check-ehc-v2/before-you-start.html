Before you start

If your certificate was issued in Northern Ireland, you should use the Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs (DAERA) service instead.

To find out where your certificate was issued, check the 12 digit number. Certificates issued in Northern Ireland start with a number 3.
